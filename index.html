Hows and whys

The Webpage was made entirely in github with no Java integration. It's made to be simple in a glance. Links don't make new window, so left click to make a new tab is required to stay on the page. From a stylistic standpoint, the SVG of the circle is supposed to emulate the sun, to give an oddly satisfying feeling. (at least to me) The Canvas blocks on the listing is to make them sandwiched, I had debated extending it to the end of the page, but it looked odd. Image choice was an attempt to be funny with the beer crossed out. (It's still gross.) The video and audio are supposed to go together as a small joke. The video was of yours truly in the bathroom with my newest silly hat I got in Orlando. It doubled as a video and a way to show my face. I shook my head to make the tail look like it's swimming. This is to go with the audio of water splashing to make the swimming joke land. Alternatively, I considered the Jaws theme but the video was too short for it to go any good with it.
